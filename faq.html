Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

This FAQ Page will be updated with new information before the event starts.
Updated as of Oct 12, 2015 6:41 AM

Can we have students from other universities in our team?
Yes. As long as they are considered a student in their respective schools. The hackercup team will only coordinate with the registered team leader who will be considered liable in sharing the information with the rest of his/her team members.

How will we know if we are already registered for the event?
You will be informed via email if your team have successfully registered as participants. If you haven't, you may have submitted incomplete requirements so double-check first before submitting.

Are we required to attend the orientation and workshops?
There must be at least one (1) team representative to attend the orientation. We highly encourage you to attend our workshops for you to familiarize yourself with the different technologies that you can use during the hackathon.

Is there a registration fee for DLSU HackerCup 2015?
Nope, DLSU HackerCup 2015 is free of charge! (Transportation fees do not apply)

My question is not listed here. How can I contact you?
If you have questions not mentioned above, please send us an email at dlsuhackercup@gmail.com.

Can graduate students apply?
Unfortunately, only undergraduate students can join the Hackercup.

Can we bring food?
While food will be provided, you can bring your own food as well. Keep in mind that you cannot eat inside the Multipurpose Room. (ex. candies and water)

Can we bring other people to the event?
No, you cannot. DLSU HackerCup 2015 is only open to registered participants and organizers.

Is it okay if one of us cannot stay at the campus overnight?
Yes, team members who cannot stay overnight can leave the campus before 10 pm and return after 6 am the next day.

Are there parking spots near the venue where I can park my car overnight?
There are parking spots available. A document regarding parking will be published soon.
