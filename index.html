The longest running student hackathon is now on it's 4th year.

The DLSU Hackercup now opens its doors for all Philippine students to challenge themselves on a 48-hour competition of turning caffeine, food, energy drinks, and fun into technological solutions with social impact.

Eligibility

Students can form teams of 3 - 5 members from any backgrounds and any schools. These students should be enrolled for the current academic year or considered by their respective schools as a student until December 2015. You may Click here to register your team and create a DevPost account.

Walk-ins are not accepted. For member substitions, please email support@dlsuhackercup.com

Competition

Venue: De La Salle University

Teams will be building mobile, web, or hardware-based applications. Pre-made applications are not allowed.
