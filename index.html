DLSU HackerCup, an annual student hackathon, is organized by DLSU College of Computer Studies and DLSU Center for Complexity and Emerging Technologies. Participants are to challenge themselves on a 24-hour competition of turning caffeine, food, energy drinks, and fun into technological solutions with social impact.

Eligibility

Students can form teams of 3 - 5 members from any backgrounds as long as they belong to the same institution. These students should be enrolled for the current academic year or considered as a student for the current semester. You may Click Here to register.

Walk-ins are not accepted. For member substitutions and other membership inquiries, please email dlsuhackercup@gmail.com
