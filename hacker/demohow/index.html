How To
Demo

To better enable teams to demonstrate their app to judges and each other, we have decided to follow a science fair type of demo. In this type of demo, not only will people be able to see the app up close and personal, but they will also be able to feel and interact with the app as well. The usual question and answer format now turns into conversations. Your pitch will still be important but remember it's your app that truly matters!!

  1. The judging will be conducted in a manner similar to a science fair.
  2. Teams will be asked to set-up their booth on their table.
  3. Judges will be visiting each of the booths together.
  4. Each team will have 5 minutes to present their work to the panel of judges.
  5. The use of powerpoint presentations and the like will not be allowed, instead teams must present the app itself, and allow judges to interact with them as well. Participants will be allowed to roam around and visit the booths of others during this time, however, it is expected that at least 2 members be present at their booths at all times.
  6. During judging, all members must be present.
  7. After visiting all the booths, the judges will select the top 3 teams for each category to present on stage for everyone to see
  8. After their presentation, the winner of each category will then be chosen and awarded.
